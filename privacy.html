Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026 · hello@zmove.io

1. Who we are

Z-Move is an agentless multi-cloud migration platform operated by Adilson Camenha ("we", "us", "our"). Our website is https://zmove.io.

2. What data we collect

We collect only the minimum necessary to operate and improve Z-Move:

  • Contact form: Name, work email, company, and migration details you voluntarily submit.
  • Analytics: We use Plausible Analytics — a privacy-first, cookieless analytics service. No cookies are set, no personal data is collected, and IP addresses are never stored. Plausible is GDPR-compliant by design.
  • Server logs: Standard web server logs (IP address, browser, referring page) retained for up to 30 days for security and debugging.

3. How we use your data

  • To respond to demo requests and enquiries submitted through our contact form.
  • To understand aggregate usage patterns via cookieless analytics (no individual tracking).
  • To maintain the security and performance of our service.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data storage and security

Contact form submissions are processed by Formspree (USA), which is GDPR-compliant. We retain form data for as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry, and no longer than 12 months.

We apply industry-standard security measures including HTTPS, HSTS, and strict Content Security Policy headers.

5. Cookies

This website uses no cookies. Our analytics provider (Plausible) operates without cookies and does not track individual users across sessions or sites.

6. Your rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Object to processing or request restriction of processing.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@zmove.io.

7. Third-party services

  • Plausible Analytics Privacy Policy
  • Formspree Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts (fonts loaded from fonts.googleapis.com — see Google Privacy Policy)

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page will always reflect the latest version. Continued use of Z-Move after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact
